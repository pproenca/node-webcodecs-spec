# 9.9.2. Constructors

`VideoColorSpace(init)`

1.  Let c be a new [`VideoColorSpace`](#videocolorspace) object, initialized as follows:
    1.  Assign `init.primaries` to [`[[primaries]]`](#dom-videocolorspace-primaries-slot).
    2.  Assign `init.transfer` to [`[[transfer]]`](#dom-videocolorspace-transfer-slot).
    3.  Assign `init.matrix` to [`[[matrix]]`](#dom-videocolorspace-matrix-slot).
    4.  Assign `init.fullRange` to [`[[full range]]`](#dom-videocolorspace-full-range-slot).

2.  Return c.
